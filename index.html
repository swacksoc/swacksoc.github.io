A place for your passions, whatever they may be. Learn, create, hack!

Swack aims to provide a fun and collaborative space for students to work on projects. We run weekly meets on Wednesdays, where members can come to discuss projects, tech, crafts, alpacas, and anything in between.
We also have a Discord server with an active and fun community.

For our meets we have a code of conduct we ask members to follow. It can be found here.
