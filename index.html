swan_hack aims to provide a fun and collaborative space for students to work on projects. We run weekly meets on Wednesdays where members can come to work on projects that interest them. We provide support for students working on programming and technology related coursework, as well as offering programming lessons and talks on exciting new areas of tech.


For our meets we have a code of conduct we ask members to follow. It can be found here.
